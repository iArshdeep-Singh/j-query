Add/Remove Elements

prepend() method inserts content at the beginning of the selected elements

append() method inserts content at the end of the selected elements

after() method inserts content after the selected elements

before() method inserts content before the selected elements

remove() removes the content, the selected element and its child elements

empty() removes the content and the child elements from the selected element
